Fucking Texts

Hi, welcome to Fucking Texts. This little utility will give you some text stats. It's all done client side so no need to worry about me storing any information (like I want to read your bullshit anyway). Also, it's just for iPhones right now because that's what I have. I'll look up Android later so you fucks will just have to wait.

The iPhone text DB is a file named '3d0d7e5fb2ce288813306e4d4636395e047a3d28' and it's located in your itunes backup directory.

Windows: \Users\(username)\AppData\Roaming\Apple Computer\MobileSync\Backup\(most recent backup)\3d\
Mac: ~/Library/Application Support/MobileSync/Backup/(most recent backup)/3d/
